5. Description of the App & Features

Need4Me Filters & Backgrounds is an Android photo editing application that offers photo filters, beauty background replacement, scan-line beauty filters, and AR camera lenses. The App operates on a freemium model with a free tier and a paid Premium subscription.

The App's main sections are:

  • Photo Filters: Apply artistic and beauty filters to your photos
  • Beauty Backgrounds: Replace your photo background using on-device AI (ML Kit Selfie Segmentation)
  • Scan Line Filters: Beauty filters applied with a dramatic animated sweep effect
  • Snap Camera Kit: Live AR lenses powered by Snap Inc.'s Camera Kit SDK

The App is available exclusively on Android through the Google Play Store. Features, content, and availability may change over time as described in Section 17.
