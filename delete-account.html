Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains exactly how to delete your account, what data is removed, what is kept, and your privacy rights.

⛔
This Action Is Permanent and Cannot Be Undone

Once your account is deleted, the following cannot be recovered:

  • Your login credentials and account access
  • Your subscription history and premium status
  • Any in-app preferences or settings

Before deleting, please cancel your active Google Play subscription first to avoid future charges — account deletion does NOT automatically cancel billing. See the Subscription section below.

⚠️ Step 0 — Cancel Your Subscription First!

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription. You must cancel it separately or you will continue to be charged even after your account is deleted.

  • Open Google Play Store On your Android device, open the Google Play Store app.
  • Tap Your Profile Icon Tap your profile photo in the top right corner of the Play Store.
  • Go to Payments & Subscriptions → Subscriptions Tap "Payments & subscriptions" then tap "Subscriptions" to see your active subscriptions.
  • Find Need4Me Filters & Backgrounds → Cancel Tap on Need4Me, then tap "Cancel subscription" and confirm. You keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period.
  • Confirm Cancellation You should receive a confirmation email from Google Play. After this, you can safely delete your account.
⏱ Important Timing Note

Cancel your subscription at least 24 hours before your next billing date. After cancellation, you retain Premium access through the end of your paid period.

Option 1 — Delete In the App (Fastest)

The quickest way to delete your account is directly from the Need4Me app:

  • Open Need4Me Filters & Backgrounds Launch the app on your Android device and sign in if needed.
  • Tap the Profile Icon Tap the Profile icon at the bottom of the home screen.
  • Open Settings Inside your Profile page, tap the Settings icon or "Settings" option.
  • Scroll Down and Tap "Delete Account" Scroll to the bottom of Settings and tap Delete Account.
  • Confirm Deletion A confirmation dialog will appear. Read it carefully and confirm. This action cannot be reversed.
  • Done Your account will be permanently deleted and you will be signed out immediately. Personal data is fully removed within 30 days.

Option 2 — Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion by email, we will process your request within 30 days of receiving it. We may need to verify your identity before processing.

Your Privacy Rights — Data Requests

You have privacy rights beyond account deletion. Depending on your location, you may also request:

📋 Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you.

✏️ Right to Correction

Request that we correct any inaccurate personal information.

📦 Right to Data Portability

Request your personal data in a machine-readable format.

🚫 Right to Opt Out

California residents: opt out of any sharing of personal information (we don't sell it, but you have this right).

🛑 Right to Restrict Processing

EU/UK residents: request that we restrict how we process your data while a dispute is resolved.

🏛️ Right to Complain

EU residents: lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. UK residents: contact the ICO at ico.org.uk.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at need4mebeautyfast@gmail.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days (or sooner if required by applicable law). We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

Under California law, you have the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of the sale/sharing of personal information. Use subject line "California Privacy Request" when emailing us. We will respond within 45 days as required by CCPA.

Partial Data Deletion (Without Deleting Your Account)

Currently, the App does not offer a self-service tool to delete specific types of data without deleting your full account. However, you may email us at any time to request deletion of specific data (for example, requesting that Firebase Analytics data associated with your account be deleted), and we will process your request within 30 days to the extent technically feasible.

For partial data deletion requests, email need4mebeautyfast@gmail.com with the subject line "Partial Data Deletion Request" and describe what data you would like removed.
