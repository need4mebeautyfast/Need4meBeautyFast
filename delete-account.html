Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Follow the steps below to request deletion of your account and associated data.

⚠️
This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Once your account is deleted, your login credentials, subscription history, and all associated data will be permanently removed. This cannot be reversed. If you have an active Premium subscription, please cancel it first through Google Play to avoid future charges.

Option 1 — Delete Directly In the App

The fastest way to delete your account is from within the Need4Me BeautyFast app:

  • Open the App Launch Need4Me BeautyFast on your Android device.
  • Go to Your Profile Tap the Profile icon at the bottom of the screen.
  • Open Settings Tap Settings inside your profile page.
  • Tap "Delete Account" Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account. Confirm when prompted.
  • Done Your account will be permanently deleted and you will be signed out immediately.

Option 2 — Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us. We will process your request within 30 days.


📧 Send Us a Deletion Request

Tap the button below to open a pre-filled email. Include the email address associated with your Need4Me BeautyFast account.

✉️ Email Deletion Request need4mebeautyfast@gmail.com

Please include in your email:

  • The email address linked to your Need4Me BeautyFast account
  • Subject line: "Delete My Account – Need4Me BeautyFast"
  • A brief statement that you wish to permanently delete your account
⏱ We respond within 30 days

What Data Is Deleted vs. Kept

When your account is deleted, here is what happens to your data:


Data Type Action Timeframe
Account email & password 🗑️ Permanently deleted Immediately
Profile information 🗑️ Permanently deleted Immediately
Subscription history 🗑️ Permanently deleted Within 30 days
Firebase analytics data 🗑️ Anonymized / deleted Within 30 days
Your photos ✅ Never stored on our servers — photos stay on your device only N/A
Payment records 📋 Retained for legal/tax compliance Kept up to 7 years (required by law)

⚠️ Cancel Your Subscription First!

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription. To avoid being charged after account deletion:

  • Open Google Play Store On your Android device.
  • Go to Account → Subscriptions Tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  • Find Need4Me BeautyFast → Cancel Tap Cancel Subscription and confirm. You keep Premium access until the end of your billing period.
